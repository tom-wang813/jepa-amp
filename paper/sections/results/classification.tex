JEPA-AMP achieves AUROC~\textbf{0.958} and MCC~\textbf{0.802}, approaching but
not surpassing the AMPlify ensemble (AUROC~0.984) or ESM-2 (AUROC~0.963) when
trained on identical data.
On the APD3 independent transfer set (556 sequences), JEPA-AMP achieves
AUROC~\textbf{0.944} and MCC~\textbf{0.758} (CIs $[0.931, 0.957]$ and
$[0.724, 0.793]$), above the larger balanced-data probe (AUROC~0.880).

\finding{AMP-specific JEPA pre-training produces a useful compact backbone for
classification without surpassing the specialised AMPlify ensemble or ESM-2.
This result supports representation \emph{usability}, not model superiority.}
