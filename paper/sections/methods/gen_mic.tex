\subsection{MIC-Conditioned Generation}
\label{sec:mic_gen}

\paragraph{Extended condition vector.}
The AR v4 decoder is extended to a 43-dimensional condition vector:
\begin{equation}
  \mathbf{c} =
  \underbrace{[\ell/50,\;\tanh(q/5),\;\tanh(h)]}_{\text{physchem }(3)}
  \;\|\;
  \underbrace{[m_1,\ldots,m_{20}]}_{\text{MIC values }(20)}
  \;\|\;
  \underbrace{[\mu_1,\ldots,\mu_{20}]}_{\text{MIC mask }(20)},
\end{equation}
where $m_i$ is the target $\log_2\text{MIC}$ and $\mu_i \in \{0,1\}$
indicates whether species $i$ is constrained.
Per-bacterium CFG dropout is applied during training.

\paragraph{Evaluation.}
Generated sequences are evaluated only through computational MIC scorers
(JEPA oracle and independently trained ESM-2 scorer) and
physicochemical filters; no wet-lab MIC, hemolysis, or cytotoxicity data
are claimed in this study.
